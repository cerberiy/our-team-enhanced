The best way to display your employees, team members, or any type of list. Comes with multiple templates & drag & drop re-order functionality. Ready out of the box! Display your team members in a professional and modern style.

== Description ==

[View Demo](http://smartcatdesign.net/our-team-showcase/)
This demo shows you the grid style template. This is 1 of 3 templates you can use.

* This plugin allows you to add, edit, search and display your team members on any page, or in a widget
quickly and easily. 
* It comes with a couple of different styles to choose from.(coming soon)
* Re-order team members with a simple drag & drop.
* output your team members anywhere with the shortcode [our-team]

[youtube http://youtu.be/upV_fevCh_s]

== Screenshots ==
1. Plugin includes Grid boxes, Grid Circular and sidebar templates
2. Easy edit with wysiwyg editor, featured image, and some extra options
3. Drag & Drop re-order of memebers

== Installation ==
1. Download the plugin, then upload the zipped file to your site from the WordPress dashboard plugin uploader menu
2. OR - Upload the unpacked folder folder via FTP into /wp-content/plugins
